All Skilljar modules are self-paced, available online, and free of charge. Employees complete these at their own pace before attending instructor-led labs. Each completed course awards a vendor-recognised Anthropic certificate.

Instructor-Led · On-site

Hands-On Labs & Sessions

All lab sessions are instructor-led and conducted on-site using real Allianz environment, scenarios, and data. Employees work through practical exercises that apply directly to their day-to-day role.

View the session schedule, check available cohort dates, and register directly from the Onsite Session Schedule & Registration tab above.

How Completion is Tracked

Completion is tracked at the module level. Each employee must complete both components of their assigned track — Skilljar online courses and instructor-led lab sessions — before their training is considered fully complete.

Skilljar Modules — Certificate Submission

Upon completing each Skilljar course, Anthropic automatically issues a vendor-recognised certificate. These certificates are issued directly by Anthropic and recognised across the Allianz Group.

Action required: Save your certificate immediately after each Skilljar course. You are required to upload it as proof of completion. Certificates not saved or submitted will not be counted.
Submit Certificate ↗
Lab Sessions — Attendance Confirmation

Attendance is recorded by the session facilitator. Employees do not need to take any additional action — attendance is marked based on confirmed registration and presence at the session.

Session recordings will be made available after each lab for reference only. Viewing a recording does not count as attendance.
